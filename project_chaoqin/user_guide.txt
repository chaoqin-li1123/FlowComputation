Welcome to flow_computation 0.0.0!
Click the "source file" button to browse the local the computer and find the parameter file.("pq.txt", "pq_pv.txt").
The image and report will be displayed on window.
Image(power_network.jpg) and report(flow.report) are also saved in the same directory as the script.
Check box "parallelism?" decide whether to use multiprocess to do the computation. 
If you want to switch file, just click the "source file" button and select another file.
Click the "close" button to shut down the window and terminate the process.
